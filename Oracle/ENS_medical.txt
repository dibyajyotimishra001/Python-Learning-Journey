Take a deep breath. The medical field is vast, and an MBBS degree is not the only way to build a successful and impactful career in healthcare.
Option 1: If you believe you can crack it with another year of dedicated study, a drop year is a common and acceptable choice for NEET aspirants.

Option 2: Explore Allied Health Sciences. Careers in B.Sc. Nursing, Physiotherapy, Radiology, and Optometry are in massive demand globally.

Option 3: Consider Research and Pharmaceuticals. B.Pharm, Pharm.D, or B.Sc. in Biotechnology/Microbiology offer excellent opportunities in research, drug development, and laboratories.

Actionable Step: Evaluate your financial and emotional readiness for a drop year versus joining an allied field. Both paths are highly respected and offer stable futures.
